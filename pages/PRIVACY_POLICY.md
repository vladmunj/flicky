# Privacy Policy for Flicky Application

**Last Updated:** February 7, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Flicky ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we process information when you use our mobile application.

**Important:** The Flicky application does not collect, store, or transmit users' personal data.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Flicky does not collect the following types of data:
- Name, email address, phone number
- Registration or authentication data
- Geolocation
- Files and media files from your device
- Contacts
- Viewing history or user preferences
- Any other personally identifiable information

## 3. Information Used by the Application

### 3.1. Device Language

The application uses your device's language to display the interface in the appropriate language (Russian or English). This information is processed only locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or third parties.

### 3.2. Image Caching

The application caches movie and TV show poster images on your device to improve performance. This data is stored only locally and is automatically managed by the Android system.

## 4. Use of Third-Party Services

Flicky uses the following third-party APIs to provide information about movies and TV shows:

### 4.1. The Movie Database (TMDb)

- **Service:** https://www.themoviedb.org/
- **Usage:** Obtaining information about movies, TV shows, cast, images, and ratings
- **Data Transmitted:** API requests contain only search queries and filtering parameters (genre, year, rating, country). Personal data is not transmitted.
- **TMDb Privacy Policy:** https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

### 4.2. OMDb API

- **Service:** https://www.omdbapi.com/
- **Usage:** Obtaining IMDb ratings for movies
- **Data Transmitted:** Only movie identifiers (IMDb ID). Personal data is not transmitted.
- **OMDb Privacy Policy:** https://www.omdbapi.com/legal.htm

### 4.3. Kinopoisk Unofficial API

- **Service:** https://kinopoiskapiunofficial.tech/
- **Usage:** Obtaining Kinopoisk ratings for movies
- **Data Transmitted:** Only movie identifiers. Personal data is not transmitted.
- **Privacy Policy:** See service documentation

### 4.4. Streaming Platforms

The application may open external links to streaming platforms (Netflix, Amazon Prime, etc.) to view information about content availability. When following these links, the privacy policy of the respective platform applies.

## 5. Application Permissions

Flicky requests the following permissions:

### 5.1. Internet (INTERNET)

- **Purpose:** Required to obtain data about movies and TV shows from third-party APIs
- **Usage:** Only for API requests to services listed in section 4

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information:
- All API requests are made through secure HTTPS connections
- API keys are stored locally on the device and are not transmitted to third parties
- The application does not save search history or user preferences

## 7. Children

Flicky is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 9. Your Rights

- Right to rectification of data
- Right to erasure of data
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to data portability

If you have questions about your rights, please contact us.
